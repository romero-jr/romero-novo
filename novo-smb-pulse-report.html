04 — Methodology

How we know.

250K+businesses in community
The Novo Pulse draws on a community of more than 250,000 independent businesses, a quarterly survey of 4,200 owners weighted to reflect U.S. industry and geography, and aggregated, anonymized signals from what the community is asking and talking about. No individual account data is ever identified or shared. Figures shown here are sample data for this prototype and clearly labeled as such.
